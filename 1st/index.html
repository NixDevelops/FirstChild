Welcome

Hello, my name is Hussein Zeaiter also known as Nix and I'm a Web Developer, well...sorta.
This is my first personal Website ever that I created from scratch, all of the content as well as the design found were coded by my own hand, which is, on the contrary to stereotypes, really fun to do.

Purpose

I built this website in order to practice and enhance what I have been studying for the past month. It's nowhere near "professional" but rather works as a tool for me to determine my weakpoints. I try to include everything I studied as well as everything I researched on the internet in the mean time, which is mostly simple yet very confusing (at first) concepts of HTML5 and CSS3, and that is because this Website was only built on those two languages. It lacks both JavaScript and the key concepts of responsive design (which means it looks "meh" on different screens ), and if you're wondering why is that, well it's because I haven't started the rest of the courses still, and I started designing literally after completing my CSS3 course by 1min.

Anyways, to those who decide to continue adventuring through my simple work, I hope you enjoy your stay, and thank you for riding along in this journey, that just began.
